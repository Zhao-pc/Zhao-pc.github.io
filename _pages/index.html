## 🔔 Latest News - *2025.05*: I have joined **Beihang University (BUAA)** as a Professor. 🎉 - *2024.10.24*: Our paper 'Miniature optical fiber photoacoustic spectroscopy gas sensor based on a 3D micro-printed planar-spiral spring optomechanical resonator' has been published in [Photoacoustics](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.pacs.2024.100657). 🎉 - *2024.09.21*: I was awarded the Best Oral Presentation Award at OFS-China 2024. 🎉 - *2024.07.10*: I was awarded the Humboldt Research Fellowship for Postdocs. 🎉 --- I am **Pengcheng Zhao**, a **Humboldt Fellow (洪堡学者)** at the Leibniz Institute of Photonic Technology (IPHT) in Germany, working in the Department of Fiber Photonics led by [Prof. Markus Schmidt](https://www.leibniz-ipht.de/en/departments/fiber-photonics/). Prior to this, I was a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU), collaborating with [Prof. Wei Jin](https://www.polyu.edu.hk/eee/people/academic-staff-and-teaching-staff/prof-jin-wei/) and [Prof. Aping Zhang](https://www.polyu.edu.hk/researchgrp/azhang/index.htm). I received my Ph.D. from Beihang University (BUAA) in 2022 and my B.S. from Jilin University (JLU) in 2015. I have received numerous fellowships and awards, including [Humboldt Research Fellowship](https://www.humboldt-foundation.de/en/connect/explore-the-humboldt-network/singleview/1240498/dr-pengcheng-zhao), ["China's Top 10 Optical Breakthroughs in 2020"](https://www.opticsjournal.net/columns/zggx?type=view&postid=PT21042600003LiOkR), [Best Oral Presentation Award](https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/vHMugK28tNrUqEpGskSMDQ) at OFS-China 2024, and Excellent Doctoral Thesis Awards of both Beijing and BUAA. ![avatar](https://pczhao.cn/images/sdjz_zsl.jpg) My research interests include **laser spectroscopy**, **fiber-optic sensors**, and **fiber-optic devices**. I have participated in four research grants, published over 10 peer-reviewed papers in journals such as ***Nature Communications***, ***Laser & Photonics Reviews***, ***Nano Letters***, and co-authored 3 national patents.
