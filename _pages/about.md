<p style="text-align: justify;">
I am <strong>Pengcheng Zhao</strong>, a <strong>Humboldt Fellow (洪堡学者)</strong> at the Leibniz Institute of Photonic Technology (德国莱布尼兹光子技术研究所) in Germany, currently working in the Department of Fiber Photonics led by <a href="https://www.leibniz-ipht.de/en/departments/fiber-photonics/">Prof. Markus Schmidt</a>. Prior to this, I was a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU), collaborating with <a href="https://www.polyu.edu.hk/eee/people/academic-staff-and-teaching-staff/prof-jin-wei/">Prof. Wei Jin</a> and <a href="https://www.polyu.edu.hk/researchgrp/azhang/index.htm">Prof. Aping Zhang</a>. I received my Ph.D. from Beihang University (BUAA) in 2022 and my B.S. from Jilin University (JLU) in 2015. Throughout my academic career, I have received numerous fellowships/scholarships and awards, including <a href="https://www.humboldt-foundation.de/en/connect/explore-the-humboldt-network/singleview/1240498/dr-pengcheng-zhao">Humboldt Research Fellowship</a>, <a href="https://www.opticsjournal.net/columns/zggx?type=view&postid=PT21042600003LiOkR">"China's Top 10 Optical Breakthroughs in 2020"</a> (<strong>2020年度中国光学十大进展</strong>), <a href="https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/vHMugK28tNrUqEpGskSMDQ">Best Oral Presentation Award</a> at OFS-China 2024, and the Excellent Doctoral Thesis Awards of both Beijing and BUAA. I will be joining BUAA as a professor this May🎉🎉🎉.
</p>

![avatar](/images/sdjz_zsl.jpg)

<p style="text-align: justify;">
My research interests include laser spectroscopy, fiber-optic sensors, and fiber-optic devices. I have participated in four research grants, published over 10 peer-reviewed papers in journals such as <strong><em>Nature Communications</em></strong>, <strong><em>Laser &amp; Photonics Reviews</em></strong>, <strong><em>Nano Letters</em></strong>, and co-authored 3 national patents. I also serve as a reviewer for international journals, such as <em>Journal of Lightwave Technology</em>, <em>Optics Letters</em>, <em>Optics Express</em>.
</p>
